Practical, hands-on woodworking skills — from basic hand tools to advanced structural constructions, taught the real Malawi way.

Introduction to the Technology Module

The Technology module is the heart of our carpentry and joinery training. Here you will learn real workshop and site skills through step-by-step projects — everything a qualified carpenter and joiner in Malawi needs to know.

We follow a practical, project-based approach aligned with TEVET competencies. You will use tools, work with timber, build components, and construct full structures. Each topic includes safety, measurements, quality checks, and finishing.

This module covers 9 main practical areas:

1. Using Hand Tools

Master all essential hand tools safely and accurately.
