Entrepreneurship – Level 1

Qualification Level One focuses on basic entrepreneurial understanding and the ability to start a small business.

1. Developing Entrepreneurial Motivation

This module helps you develop the motivation and mindset required to start and run a profitable business.

  • Conduct entrepreneurial self-assessment
  • Demonstrate personal entrepreneurial characteristics
  • Demonstrate achievement-oriented behaviour
  • Identify and solve entrepreneurial challenges

2. Starting a Profitable Business

This module teaches how to start and run a viable (successful) business — including identifying opportunities, planning, managing resources, and ensuring profit.

  • Starting a viable business
  • Running a profitable business

Module 1: Developing Entrepreneurial Motivation

Purpose

A trainee should be able to:
• Develop entrepreneurial motivation
• Build entrepreneurial characteristics
• Show achievement-oriented behaviour

Key Knowledge You Will Gain

Entrepreneur

A person who starts and manages a business, identifies opportunities, is creative, organises resources for profit, takes calculated risks, and markets products/services.

Characteristics of Successful Entrepreneurs

Clear purpose • Passion • Customer focus • Persistence • Creativity • Coordination ability • Agent of change

Entrepreneurial Values

Self-confidence • Persistence • Hard work • Independence • Time consciousness • Persuasiveness • Commitment

Entrepreneurship vs Business Management

Entrepreneurship = Innovation + Risk + New ventures
Business Management = Planning + Organising + Existing systems

Benefits to the Economy

Creates employment • Improves living standards • Contributes to GDP • Promotes exports • Supports infrastructure

Self-Assessment Methods

Questionnaires • Interviews • Personality tests • Self-appraisal

Level 1 entrepreneurship mainly teaches the entrepreneurial mindset, characteristics of successful entrepreneurs, self-assessment, and the basics of starting a business — perfect for turning your carpentry skills into a sustainable workshop or site business in Malawi.
